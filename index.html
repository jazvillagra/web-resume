About me

I'm currently a senior year student of Computer Science Degree in Asunción, Paraguay. I've been working in Sodep S.A. since March 2016 and am currently a Web Developer and DevOps Team Leader.

I only need three things to survive in this world: coffee, a good book and my sisters :)

Why am I fit for the Campaign Developer Role?

I am a highly motivated, result-oriented person, willing to go an extra mile to reach our goals, that can learn along the way easily. I’m not afraid of new or unknown working fields or environments and enjoy finding problems to solve in any way I can.

I believe that technical skills are not only acquired with books and manuals, but with experimenting things with a problem to find the best (and fastest, if that’s the case) possible solution, with quality and effectiveness.

Throughout the past four years I developed many of the skills for web development that are required for this role, such as Javascript, ReactJS, Node.js and HTML5+CSS3+Bootstrap, along with database and infrastructure management, code integration and application security with a strong set of tools.

Why do I want to join your team?

When I was a little girl, I always wanted to be a superhero. Then I grew up and realized that my real dream was to help “save the world”, and everything I’ve done since then has been based on this. After finding the career I love (Computer Science & Engineering), I wondered how this could help me get closer to that old dream of mine. So, I made it a goal to find project with which I could make a difference, even if it was a small one, that involved my career in some way.

But lately, all projects I’ve taken part of at my current job, are not really related to my goals, so I decided to look for projects or jobs that actually make a difference.

Then I came across Raisely’s Campaign Developer Role, and after looking into what this organization does, there’s no doubt in my mind that this is a project that I would be delighted to be a part of and I would do my best at, because your mission and your values are exactly what I believe in.
